## Key findings — must-fix corrections vs. current deck

1. **No "THC Direct" master distributor exists** — the IL hemp-bev channel is fragmented (direct-brand WYNK/Cann + specialty wholesalers like Empire Distributors + traditional liquor wholesalers adding hemp SKUs). Deck should describe as "multi-source emerging category," not name a single distributor.
2. **Eby-Brown is now Core-Mark under PFG** (Sept 2022 consolidation). Use "Eby-Brown / Core-Mark (PFG)" in deck for accuracy.
3. **Reyes / CBS carries Constellation in Chicago** — Constellation is not a separate delivering entity; orders flow through Reyes Beverage Group (which operates Chicago Beverage Systems). Current deck label "Constellation Beer Sales / CBS" needs a footnote.

## 1. Breakthru Beverage Illinois (BBG)

- Cicero, IL DC. Formed Jan 1 2016 from Charmer Sunbelt + Wirtz Beverage IL merger.
- **Spirits:** Diageo (Smirnoff · Johnnie Walker · Crown Royal · Don Julio · Captain Morgan · Tanqueray · Bulleit) · Brown-Forman (Jack Daniel's · Woodford · Old Forester · el Jimador) · Moët Hennessy (Hennessy · Belvedere · Glenmorangie) · William Grant (Glenfiddich · Hendrick's · Tullamore D.E.W.) · Tito's · Barr Hill Gin
- **Wine:** E. & J. Gallo · O'Neill · Duckhorn · Treasury Wine Estates (19 Crimes · Sterling · Beringer)
- **Beer (LIMITED IL — most beer book sold to Lakeshore 2020).** Some craft remnants only.
- **RTD/non-alc:** Red Bull (select markets) · Keurig Dr Pepper · Recess CBD seltzers
- **Ops:** Net 30 · weekly delivery in Chicago · small case/bottle minimum
- **Fit:** Strong — must-have spirits primary for bourbon/vodka/tequila SKUs (>50% of liquor revenue at Cubs-adjacent store).

## 7. Hemp-Derived Delta-9 THC Beverages — multi-source channel

- **Status:** No single "THC Direct" master distributor identified. IL hemp-bev reaches retail via mix of: (a) brand-direct shipping where state law allows · (b) specialty wholesalers like **Empire Distributors** (carries hemp-derived bev + gummies for wholesale) · (c) traditional liquor distributors adding hemp SKUs.
- **Brands sold in IL liquor retail (Binny's 280+ THC SKUs):**
  - **WYNK** — hemp-derived Δ9 seltzers (2.5mg · 5mg) + lemonades (10mg) · 1:1 THC:CBD · Juicy Mango · Lime Twist · Black Cherry · ships to 39 states · retail in 19 incl IL
  - **Cann** — microdose THC/CBD social tonics
  - **Cheech & Chong** branded THC beverages
  - **Crescent 9** THC seltzer
- **Cresco Labs note:** Their regulated cannabis (Sunnyside dispensaries) is **separate** from the hemp-beverage channel and CANNOT be sold in liquor stores.
- **Regulatory context (IL):** SB 1766 (2025) introduced beverage-specific THC tax + license model. Track Illinois Licensed Beverage Association compliance updates.
- **Ops:** Brand-direct ordering · 21+ retail-only · Net 30 typical · 2–5 case minimums per SKU
- **Fit:** Strong category fit for Wrigleyville sober-curious 25–40 demographic. Start with 4–6 WYNK + 2 Cann SKUs; scale on velocity. **Regulatory volatility = high.**
