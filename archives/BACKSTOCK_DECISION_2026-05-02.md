## CRITICAL — needs your decision before I build the Backstock slide

### 1. AFTER plan has zero spirits category, but $3K of spirits is in inventory

The Downstairs Office holds an entire spirits library (Patron Silver, Tito's, Bacardi, Skrewball, Tanqueray, Bombay Sapphire, Tullamore Dew, Fireball, Skol, Seagram's, Gordon's, Kahlua, Malibu, Skyy, Maker's Mark, Southern Comfort, Seagram's VO, Early Times). None match the AFTER plan because the repositioning deck currently shows BC-1 back wall as "tequila / canned cocktails / vape" — but no shelf-by-shelf spirits assortment.

**Decision:**
- (a) **Keep all spirits** — add a "Well Spirits" sub-section to BC-1 in the AFTER plan, restock with this backstock. ~$3K saved.
- (b) **Curate** — pick the 8-10 SKUs the new bodega bar would actually want (Tito's, Patron, Bombay, Tanqueray) and liquidate the rest (Skol, Early Times, Seagram's VO).
- (c) **Liquidate all** — sell the entire spirits library at clearance, exclude from the business sale.

### 2. Basement wine cellar (~$20K) needs a physical walk-through

The wine cellar zone (basement crate-rack room, est. 200+ bottles) has labels not legible from the photo set. Most bottles are in dusty milk crates suggesting long-term storage. Three possibilities:
- (a) **Inventory** — premium aged wine that should transfer with business sale
- (b) **Personal collection** — owner's private cellar, not for resale
- (c) **Spoiled** — temperature/light damage from decades of basement storage

**Decision:** physical walk-through with flashlight + label-by-label check before any code is written or seller is told a number.

### 3. Modelo Especial / Coors Light / Pacifico appear on BOTH KEEP and DROP lists

Multiple agents flagged this. Looking at the active SKU list, these appear in:
- AFTER plan (kept as part of D11 / D7-D10 / D15 beer-wall assortment)
- Schedule A (some sub-formats coded as removed — e.g. Coors Light 30-pk dropped, Coors Light 18-pk kept)

**This is real ambiguity in the deck**, not an audit bug. The KEEP/DROP per-format split needs to be unambiguous. Probably: keep the 6/12/18-pk formats, drop the 30-pks. I can fix this in a separate Phase 7 cleanup.

### 4. AriZona / Gatorade flavor splits — need format/flavor-level clarity

- AriZona: Mucho Mango, Watermelon, Green Tea, Half & Half are ALL in basement backstock — but Schedule A drops some flavors and AFTER keeps others. Need flavor-by-flavor decision.
