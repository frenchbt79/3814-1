## Concerns / notes for owner

1. **Coors Light is the biggest DROP exposure** (~$155 across 12-pk btl, 18-pk can, 24-pk can floor stacks). It's prominently featured in floor stacking, suggesting this is high-velocity SKU that the AFTER plan still removes — confirm with owner whether to liquidate or sell through.
2. **Modelo Especial and Pacifico appear on BOTH lists** (active_skus.txt has them in both AFTER and Schedule A). Need owner clarification before counting these as KEEP or DROP — defaulted to REVIEW.
3. **Bud Light is on Schedule A but Budweiser is KEEP** — easy to confuse during pull. Tagging recommended.
4. **Center "LOTSA Pop" chrome rack** appears to function as a display/staging fixture rather than pure backstock; the champagne bottles and energy 4-pks on top are display product. Photos 01 + 07 only show partial top contents — counts here are conservative.
5. **No wine on shelves** — refrigerated wine implied by the cooler description was not visible in any of the 7 photos. The sparkling bottles on the LOTSA rack are the only wine-adjacent SKUs.
6. **Cardboard sheets propped against center rack** (Photos 01, 06, 07) suggest active receiving — counts may be transient.
7. **Photo 05 freezer-fan wall** shows ~half-empty right-side wire shelving (4 empty bays), consistent with an under-utilized variety/craft staging area.
