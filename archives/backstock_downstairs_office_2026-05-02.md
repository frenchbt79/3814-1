## Owner-decision summary (for review pass)

The downstairs office holds the entire **back-of-house spirits library** for the existing business. The AFTER plan is a beer/wine/seltzer-forward bodega with **no spirits category** in either KEEP or DROP lists — this means every bottle in this room is "REVIEW" by definition rather than "DROP."

**Three live options for owner:**
1. **Liquidate full spirits library** at wholesale or discount to a wholesaler / nearby bar — frees ~$3K cash and clears the closet for dry/paper backstock that supports the new format.
2. **Retain a curated cocktail-mixer subset** (Tito's, Patron Silver, Maker's Mark, Tanqueray, Bombay Sapphire, Malibu, Fireball) for a small under-counter "spirits ledge" if the AFTER plan adds a 6-SKU well-spirits shelf at the front gondola endcap. Estimated retain value: ~$900.
3. **Keep as-is** if owner has a private use case (catering, side bar, personal). Mark room as "non-retail backstock" in the deck.

**No SKU here appears in either active_skus.txt list** — the spirits assortment is entirely orthogonal to the new floorplan SKU set as currently scoped.

## Methodology notes

- Counts are best-view-wins per shelf group; photo overlaps verified before summing.
- Many bottles partially obscured by front-row bottles — back rows estimated conservatively.
- Wholesale prices use industry-standard wholesale ranges since no SKU matched index.html cost data:
  - Value vodka 1.75L: $15-22
  - Premium vodka 750mL: $15-30
  - Entry whiskey 1.75L: $18-25
  - Premium tequila 750mL: $35-40
  - Liqueurs 750mL: $18-25
- Mini bottles (50mL) treated as nominal value.
- Unreadable bottles given conservative mid-range estimates.
