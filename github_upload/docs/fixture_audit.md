## Tier organization notes (apply where possible)

**CSD doors (D1):** 20oz btl top → 12oz cans mid → 12-pk cans low → 2L kick.
**Beer doors:** 6-pks + premium top → 12-pks mid → singles low → 30-pks/cases kick.
**Energy + water (D3):** Energy top 3 shelves, water bottom 2.
**Protein/Sport/Dairy (D4):** Protein top, sport mid, dairy bottom (heavy).
**Wine coolers:** Sparkling/Champagne top, varietal mid/low — and DON'T mix red and white in the same cooler (this is the D19 error).
